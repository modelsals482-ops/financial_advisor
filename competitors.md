## Detailed Profiles

### šéfbot.cz — HIGHEST THREAT
**Location:** Praha, Smíchov
**Website:** https://sefbot.cz
**What they do:** Custom AI chatbots for Czech companies — lead gen, appointment scheduling, sales funnels, 24/7
**Clients:** 400+ businesses
**Model:** Full managed service — analysis, development, long-term management (same model as us)
**Pricing:** Not public — "free solution proposal" as entry point
**Tech:** DeepSeek integration, faster and cheaper than GPT-4
**Target:** Broad — e-shops, recruitment, general business

**Why they're a threat:**
- Same managed service model
- Already have 400 clients — proven market
- Prague based
- Do appointment scheduling

**Our edge over them:**
- They appear to focus on lead gen / sales funnels, not appointment booking for service businesses
- No visible focus on local service businesses (vets, dentists, salons)
- No public pricing = harder for small businesses to self-qualify
- We can be more personal, local, and hyper-focused on one pain

## Market Pricing Intelligence

| Price point | Who it serves |
|---|---|
| Free – 399 CZK/month | Self-serve tools (talabot, odpovidej) — DIY |
| 2,000–5,000 CZK/month | Our target zone — affordable managed service |
| 20,000 CZK/month | bezvabot — medium businesses |
| 65,000–100,000+ CZK setup | Enterprise custom builds |

**The gap is clear:** nobody is serving small appointment-based businesses (vets, dentists, salons, physios) in Prague with a managed, affordable, Czech-language AI assistant. The cheap tools are self-serve. The managed services are expensive or enterprise-focused.

---

## The Real Competition

For a Prague vet or dentist, the actual alternatives are:
1. **Doing nothing** — missing calls, losing bookings
2. **Hiring a part-time receptionist** — 15,000–25,000 CZK/month
3. **A basic booking SaaS** (Reservio, Bookio) — no AI, no phone, client must book themselves

We beat all three on price and convenience.
