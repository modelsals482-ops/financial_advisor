## Detailed Profiles

### šéfbot.cz — HIGHEST THREAT
**Location:** Praha, Smíchov
**Website:** https://sefbot.cz
**What they do:** Custom AI chatbots for Czech companies — lead gen, appointment scheduling, sales funnels, 24/7
**Clients:** 400+ businesses
**Model:** Full managed service — analysis, development, long-term management (same model as us)
**Pricing:** Not public — "free solution proposal" as entry point
**Tech:** DeepSeek integration, faster and cheaper than GPT-4
**Target:** Broad — e-shops, recruitment, general business

**Why they're a threat:**
- Same managed service model
- Already have 400 clients — proven market
- Prague based
- Do appointment scheduling

**Our edge over them:**
- They appear to focus on lead gen / sales funnels, not appointment booking for service businesses
- No visible focus on local service businesses (vets, dentists, salons)
- No public pricing = harder for small businesses to self-qualify
- We can be more personal, local, and hyper-focused on one pain

## Indirect Competitors: Booking SaaS

These are tools a vet or dentist might already use. They are NOT AI, NOT managed — just a booking widget the client sets up themselves.

### Reservio
**Website:** https://reservio.com
**What it is:** Self-serve online booking calendar. No AI, no email handling.
**Pricing (confirmed):**
| Plan | Price | Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Free | 0 | 40 bookings/month |
| Starter | ~$10/month (~230 CZK) | 200 bookings/month |
| Standard | ~$20/month (~460 CZK) | 500 bookings/month |
| Pro | ~$40/month (~920 CZK) | Unlimited bookings |
**Why it matters:** Clients may already use it. It solves online booking ONLY — email still piles up, phone still rings. We replace the receptionist's entire communication load, Reservio just adds a booking button.

### Bookio / BookioPro
**Website:** https://bookio.com / https://bookiopro.com
**What it is:** Similar to Reservio — online booking system for salons, clinics, restaurants.
**Pricing:** Free tier + premium plans (specific CZK prices not publicly listed, similar range to Reservio based on market positioning). 30% discount for annual payment. First 3 months premium free.
**Why it matters:** Same positioning as Reservio. Used by salons and clinics. No AI, no managed service — client runs everything themselves.

**Key insight on both:** A client using Reservio still gets 50+ emails/day they handle manually. We are additive to Reservio, not a replacement. Pitch angle: "You probably already have a booking tool — we handle everything they don't."

## Market Pricing Intelligence

| Price point | Who it serves |
|---|---|
| Free – 399 CZK/month | Self-serve tools (talabot, odpovidej, Reservio Free) — DIY |
| ~230–920 CZK/month | Reservio/Bookio SaaS — booking widget only, no AI, no support |
| 3,000–5,000 CZK/month | Our target zone — full managed AI service |
| 20,000 CZK/month | bezvabot — medium businesses |
| 65,000–100,000+ CZK setup | Enterprise custom builds |

**The gap is clear:** nobody is serving small appointment-based businesses (vets, dentists, salons, physios) in Prague with a managed, affordable, Czech-language AI assistant. The cheap tools are self-serve. The managed services are expensive or enterprise-focused.

---

## The Real Competition

For a Prague vet or dentist, the actual alternatives are:
1. **Doing nothing** — missing calls, losing bookings
2. **Hiring a part-time receptionist** — 15,000–25,000 CZK/month
3. **A basic booking SaaS** (Reservio, Bookio) — ~230–920 CZK/month, no AI, no email handling, client sets up everything themselves

We beat all three on price and convenience.
