## Bot 1: Front Desk Bot — Physio & Wellness

**Status:** Ready to deploy

**Target verticals:** Physiotherapists, wellness studios, massage therapists

**Core problem it solves:** Owner is hands-on all day and can't answer phone or email. Bookings are lost to voicemail and slow replies.

**What it does:**
- Handles inbound booking requests via email and WhatsApp
- Checks availability in real time against Google Calendar
- Confirms bookings and sends reminders (24h before + 1h before)
- Handles cancellations and fills slots from a waitlist
- Answers FAQ: location, price, services, cancellation policy
- Escalates clinical or complex questions to the owner

**Key differentiator:** Recurring appointment awareness — knows if a patient books every Tuesday and proactively offers to rebook next week's slot.

**Pricing:**
- Setup fee: 8,000–10,000 CZK (one-time)
- Monthly retainer: 4,500–5,000 CZK

**Infra complexity:** Medium — Google Calendar integration required

**ICP deep-dive:** See `docs/vertical_physio.md`

## Bot 2: Front Desk Bot — Salons & Beauty

**Status:** Ready to deploy

**Target verticals:** Hair salons, nail studios, beauty salons, brow/lash studios

**Core problem it solves:** High booking volume, multiple staff members with different schedules and specialties. Owner is often behind the chair and unavailable to respond.

**What it does:**
- Handles inbound booking requests via email and WhatsApp
- Routes each booking to the right staff member based on service and availability
- Sends confirmations and reminders (24h before + 1h before)
- Upsells add-on services at confirmation: *"Your appointment is confirmed. Would you like to add a deep conditioning treatment for 200 CZK?"*
- Handles waitlist and last-minute slot filling
- Answers FAQ: price list, services, parking, cancellation policy

**Key differentiator:** Service-aware upsell at confirmation — passive revenue increase with zero extra effort from the owner.

**Pricing:**
- Setup fee: 8,000–10,000 CZK (one-time)
- Monthly retainer: 4,500–5,000 CZK

**Infra complexity:** Medium-high — multi-staff calendar management

## Bot 3: Vet Clinic Upsell Bot

**Status:** Concept stage — do not pitch until built and tested

**Target verticals:** Veterinary clinics, pet wellness centers

**Core problem it solves:** Vet clinics have high emotional attachment from clients (pet owners treat pets as family members) but zero post-visit monetization beyond the appointment fee. Product recommendations sit unused on the shelf.

**What it does:**
- Post-appointment follow-up message: *"Following Maxík's visit today, Dr. Novák recommends [product]. Order with free delivery →"*
- Vaccination and checkup reminders: *"Maxík's annual vaccination is due next month — shall we book it now?"*
- Handles inbound booking requests (same as Front Desk Bot)
- Health tips follow-up relevant to the specific visit type (post-surgery, dental, weight management)
- Product catalogue integration: bot knows what the clinic stocks and recommends based on visit type

**Key differentiator:** Leverages the emotional bond between owner and pet. Conversion rates on product recommendations in this vertical are significantly higher than generic e-commerce. The vet takes a margin on product sales — this creates a new revenue stream, not just booking efficiency.

**Why this vertical is different:**
- Clients with pets treat their animals like children — willingness to spend is high and emotionally driven
- A single well-timed recommendation (e.g., post-surgery joint supplement) can generate a purchase worth more than the appointment itself
- Recurring check-up reminders create natural re-engagement loops

**Pricing (TBD):**
- Setup fee: likely 10,000–15,000 CZK (higher complexity)
- Monthly retainer: likely 6,000–8,000 CZK
- Potential revenue share model on product sales generated by the bot

**Infra complexity:** High — requires product catalogue integration and post-visit trigger logic
