# Client Acquisition Path — ALSflow
_Created: 2026-05-03_

---

## Phase 1 — First Contact

**Goal:** Get them curious enough to agree to a 20-min meeting. Nothing more.

**Channels:** Instagram DM, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, in-person

**Message template (WhatsApp/IG):**
> *"Zdravím, jmenuji se Jakub. Všiml jsem si, že máte salon a přemýšlel jsem — kolik termínů vám ujede, protože zrovna pracujete a nemůžete zdvihnout telefon nebo odpovědět na mail? Mám systém, který to řeší automaticky. Dal byste si 20 minut na krátkou ukázku?"*

**Rules:**
- One question. One ask. No product explanation yet.
- Don't mention "AI" in the first line — triggers skepticism before trust is built
- If they ask price before the demo: *"záleží na vašem konkrétním nastavení, proto chci nejdřív pochopit jak fungujete"*

**Conversion goal:** They agree to a meeting → Phase 2.

---

## Phase 2 — In-Person Pitch (Demo Meeting)

**Goal:** Make them feel the pain of their current situation, then show it solved.

**Structure (20 min):**

| Time | What you do |
|---|---|
| 0–3 min | Ask discovery questions about missed bookings |
| 3–8 min | Run live demo — show bot receiving and confirming a booking in real time |
| 8–14 min | Explain what's included (setup, management, no tech knowledge needed) |
| 14–18 min | Handle objections |
| 18–20 min | Close or set next step |

**Discovery questions:**
- *"Kolik hovorů týdně přijdete, protože jste u zákazníka?"*
- *"Co se stane s tím zákazníkem — zkusí to znovu nebo odejde ke konkurenci?"*
- *"Máte teď někoho, kdo odpoví na maily?"*

**Rules:**
- Demo must be live and working — not slides, not a video
- Start with their pain, not your features
- Make sure the **owner** is present, not just staff
- If they're distracted or rushed, reschedule — don't rush the demo

## Phase 3 — Additional Information

**Goal:** Scope the work. Also a commitment test — if they engage, they're real.

**Happens:** Same meeting or follow-up call within 48h.

**6 questions to ask:**
1. What booking system / calendar do they use? (Google Calendar, Bookio, paper, nothing?)
2. What email client? (Gmail, Seznam, Outlook?)
3. How many types of services/appointments do they offer?
4. How many incoming emails/messages per week roughly?
5. Do they have a website? WhatsApp Business?
6. Who is the primary contact for setup — them directly or a tech-savvy employee?

**Rules:**
- 6 questions max — don't turn this into an audit
- If they can't answer basic questions, expect longer onboarding
- Never start building before you have all 6 answers

---

## Phase 4 — Custom Preferences (Proposal)

**Goal:** Deliver a personalised proposal. Show you listened.

**Deliver:** 1-page PDF (in Czech) via WhatsApp or email — not a Google Doc link.

**Proposal must include:**
- Their business name + what they do (shows you paid attention)
- Exactly which service(s) you're recommending and why
- Price: setup fee + monthly retainer
- What's included and what's not
- Timeline: *"nastavení trvá X pracovních dní"*
- Deadline: *"platnost nabídky do [date+5 days]"*
- One CTA: *"Potvrďte do [date] a začínáme tento týden"*

**Rules:**
- Give ONE recommendation — not three options (analysis paralysis kills deals)
- Optional add-on is fine, but one clear primary recommendation
- Don't request credentials or start work before setup fee is paid

---

## Phase 5 — Closing the Client

**Goal:** Get the setup fee paid. That is the close.

**Close script:**
> *"Perfektní. Pošlu vám fakturu na zálohu [X CZK] — jakmile ji uhradíte, začínáme. Nastavení trvá přibližně 5 pracovních dní a pak vám vše předám spolu s krátkým videem jak to funguje."*

**Don't ask "chcete to?" — assume the sale and move to logistics.**

**After payment:**
1. Send onboarding questionnaire (calendar access, email credentials, FAQ content)
2. Build + test workflow
3. Go live
4. Send a short Loom video showing what happens when a booking comes in

**Common objections + responses:**

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| *"Musím se poradit s manželem/kou"* | Find out who the decision-maker is earlier next time. Follow up in 48h. |
| *"Zavolám vám"* | Don't wait. Follow up in 48h: *"Jen jsem chtěl ověřit, jestli jste měl/a čas to probrat."* |
| Price pushback | Reduce scope (e.g. email bot only), never lower the rate. |
| *"Máme už Reservio"* | *"Reservio je pasivní — zákazník se musí sám najít a zarezervovat. My řešíme konverzaci, zatímco vy pracujete."* |
| *"Šéfbot to dělá taky"* | *"Šéfbot pracuje s e-shopy a recruitery. My jsme specializovaní přesně na provozovny jako ta vaše."* |

## Timing Overview

| Phase | When | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| First Contact | Anytime | 2 min (message) |
| Demo Meeting | Within 3–5 days of contact | 20 min |
| Additional Info | Same meeting or within 48h | 10–15 min |
| Proposal delivery | Within 24–48h of info gathering | — |
| Close | Within 5 days of proposal | — |

**Total cycle target: under 2 weeks from first message to paid setup fee.**
