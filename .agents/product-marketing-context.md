## Product Overview

**One-liner:** Fully managed AI automation service that handles emails, booking, and customer replies for Czech small businesses — 24/7, no tech skills required.

**What it does:** ALSflow deploys and fully manages AI bots for appointment-based local businesses. The bot handles incoming emails, books appointments, sends reminders, and answers FAQs via WhatsApp or a custom frontend. The client pays a fixed monthly fee and never touches the infrastructure — everything is set up and managed remotely.

**Product category:** AI Automation MSP (Managed Service Provider)

**Product type:** Managed service — founder owns all infrastructure, client buys outcomes not software

**Business model & pricing:**
| Product | Price |
|---|---|
| Setup fee (one-time) | 8,000–10,000 CZK |
| AI Front Desk (booking + reminders + FAQ bot) | 4,500–5,000 CZK/month |
| Email Bot (AI email replies) | 2,500–3,000 CZK/month |
| PDF Extractor (invoices / intake forms → Excel) | 1,500–2,000 CZK/month |
| Full bundle (all three) | ~7,500 CZK/month |
| Fair-use cap | 1,500 automated emails + 750 AI-booked appointments/month |
| Overage | Cost + 30% |

**Gross margin:** ~80–85%
**Break-even:** 1 client

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** The owner is the business. When they're with a client, the phone rings and no one answers. Emails pile up. Bookings fall through. Revenue leaks out of the business every day.

**Why current solutions fall short:**
- **Reservio/Bookio**: Passive — client must find the booking page themselves. Doesn't handle incoming messages or back-and-forth.
- **Hiring a receptionist**: 15,000–25,000 CZK/month, only works business hours, calls in sick, needs managing.
- **Self-serve chatbot tools (talabot, odpovidej)**: Require setup and ongoing management. Owner doesn't have time or skills.
- **Doing nothing**: The most common choice — they just accept the leakage.

**What it costs them:** Missed bookings = direct revenue loss. A salon missing 3 appointments a week at 600 CZK each = ~7,200 CZK/month gone.

**Emotional tension:** "I'm good at my job but I'm drowning in admin. I didn't open a vet clinic to answer emails all day."

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**
1. **Fully managed** — client never touches the technology. We handle setup, hosting, updates, monitoring.
2. **Appointment-business specialist** — built specifically for salons, vets, dentists, physios. Not e-shops, not recruiters.
3. **70% cheaper than a receptionist** — ~4,500–7,500 CZK/month vs. 15,000–25,000 CZK/month
4. **24/7 availability** — never misses a message, never calls in sick
5. **Public pricing** — unlike most competitors, no "contact us for a quote"
6. **Personal service** — direct relationship with founder, not a faceless SaaS dashboard

**Why customers choose us:**
- They want the outcome (no missed bookings, fewer admin tasks) without the complexity of managing software
- The price point is within reach — comparable to 1–2 missed appointments per month
- They trust a specialist over a generalist
